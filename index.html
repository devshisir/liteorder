Multi Channel Order Processing

Orders can be received from multiple channels such as Etsy, Bonanza, Ebay and automatically fulfilled by services such as FBA, etc

Dropshipping for Sellers

Sellers may import product from their Ecommerce Products and make them available for dropshipping by resellers. New orders coming from resellers will be processed as an multi channel order.

Dropshipping for Resellers

A reseller may import products into their E-commerce Web Stores, new orders will be processed by Lite Order for the sellers as a dropshipping order.

Know more about & Lite order

Lite Order is a multi channel order fulfillment service platform with dropshipping capability.

Simplify the order processing for sellers with existing product lines and fulfillment center with orders from various channels such as Ebay, Bozana, Etsy, shopify,etc

Enpowers sellers products lines with dropshipping capability using their existing fulfillment service such as FBA.

Influencers or Retail Web Stores may import products to their Ecommerce Platform such as shopify to dropship products offered by the sellers in this platform.
